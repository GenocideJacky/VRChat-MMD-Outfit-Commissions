Before You Buy


I’d like for people to exclusively take note: I make my assets in Blender 2.79. I’m mentioning this now because Blender is completely different from PMX editor, so I cannot confirm if the models I make using it will be completely flawless for MMD. I mostly make assets for VRChat avatars, so that’s what I have the most experience in.

In order to keep the model as fitting to the avatar you want it for, I may request an FBX of your model or the base you used, if you used one. This model will ONLY be used for modelling the clothes around. I am not allowed under ANY circumstances to take parts from these models. The base model will then be deleted once I have finished the commission. It is completely optional to provide this, but If declined, I cannot guarantee the asset will completely fit the model and may require further sculpting on the client’s end. Screenshots of the front and side view could also suffice, but cannot guarantee an accurate size.

Models can take anywhere from 3 days to a month to be completed. This depends on the complexity of the model and my current home situation.

I only model the outfit and you will be sent an FBX/PMX file of only that. This only applies to people who are commissioning me for outfits for already existing models. This does not apply if you own the model/have permission from the original creator to commission an outfit for the model. To find out why I operate like this, please look at my FAQ.

Please read through all of this document to make sure you completely understand what you are purchasing, and use the handy commission template at the end of the document so I can easily understand what it is you want.

Username:

Type of Commission:

This is asking what you were expecting the commission to be. The choices are:

  • Clothing Piece
  • Accessory
  • Full Outfit

If you are asking for multiple pieces of clothing in the same commission but don't think it classifies as a full outfit (eg 2/3 pieces), feel free to send the commission template multiple times, each filled in with the correct references, etc.

Use for MMD or VRChat?:

Not only will this be for which format I export to, but if you chose MMD, this will also prompt me to confirm that you are aware that I use Blender and cannot guarantee perfection for use in MMD. You’ll need to confirm that you understand this.

Dynamic Bones?:

A simple yes/no will do, but if you want to, you can have a basic description of what you want to move on the model.

MMD USER NOTE: Please remember that MMD models will come with bones in the skirts or flowy parts if you say yes to this, but I have NO idea how to make rigid bodies or joints work using blender (the things that make MMD hair and skirts flowy) and I do not know how to use PMX editor in the slightest! These bones will be useless to you unless you know how to work rigid bodies and joints in PMX editor or you plan on animating all the physics yourself, bone by bone!

My Avatar Gender:

This is just to make sure I have the correct body type, ESPECIALLY if I’m not provided a 3D avatar to mould around. This can be as simple as “Female” to as complicated as “Genderfluid” or “Non-binary, feminine aligned.” Just put your avatar’s gender, and if I need further details, I will inquire.

Clothing Image/Word Reference:

This can be either a single image of a full outfit where you point out what you want, or can be multiple images of different pieces of clothing that you have pieced together to get the desired outfit.

If you have no images, you may describe the outfit, but I will require detail. Ex. “I want a pink crop top that reaches just above the belly button with a v-neck. I want the pink to tapor into a white gradient near the bottom…” etc. This may require more back and forth and more frequent updates, but that’s only because I want it to be perfect for you.

Any Additional Information:

This is for the little details you need to add like additional accessories, a change in colour or special requests.

If you could attach a Sta.sh link or google drive link to your avatar, preferably without clothing, but if you only have it with clothing, I can make that work too. If you don’t feel comfortable sending the avatar file, that is perfectly alright! Instead, I’ll need 2 isometric screenshots of the avatar with/without clothing. One of the front straight on and the other of the side view. If the avatar has nudity, please censor this before sending the screenshots, thank you!

If you don’t feel comfortable sending either of these, that’s fine too! I will try and base a Vroid model around the gender you listed and model around that instead, but I cannot confirm if this will fit your avatar once it is completed.

FAQ


Q: Why can't you just put the outfit on the model and send me that? It's not that hard.

A: Once again, This only applies to people who do not own the model they're commissioning an outfit for. The reason why I will not send the outfit and model in the same file is because I do not feel morally right doing so. I am not here to make money off of other people's models. What people do with my outfits and who they put them on is entirely their business and not mine. If you want someone to put the outfit on the model for use in VRChat/MMD, then please either learn how or ask someone else. Thank you for understanding.

Q: Can you make a recolour of this model for me?

A: This also pertains to the first question. Recoloring someone else's model and asking for compensation is making money off of someone else's work and I shall not do so, nor do I condone doing so. However, if you have proof that you own the model or proof that you have permission, I may be able to do it for you.

Q: I don't have the money right now, but I'll have it later! Can you still take my commission?

No. This may seem harsh, but not accepting payment first thing makes me vulnerable to people who may attempt to scam me out of my time and work.

Q: Can you also model things for VRoid?

A: Yes. Not only that, if you have made the VRoid model yourself, I am more than happy to send you the model with the outfit already on instead of just sending the outfit, as I know for sure that you already own the model. (Also, no promises, but I may also be able to give you the file in .VRM or .vroid file formats, if I figure out how.)
